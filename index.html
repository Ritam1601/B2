Her Beauty

Her eyes, a haven of stars so bright,
Hold the mysteries of the night.
With lips adorned in crimson hue,
A timeless grace, forever new.

Her hair cascades in a silken stream,
Like whispers spun from a gentle dream.
In emerald hues her presence beams,
A vision woven from heaven’s seams.

A bindi rests, a mark of pride,
Framing beauty that can’t be denied.
A gaze so pure, a charm so rare,
She’s elegance beyond compare.
